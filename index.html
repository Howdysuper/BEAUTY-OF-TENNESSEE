1.) The Williamson County Fair

This fair is definitely worth your time! The reason you should visit this place is because attendees can enjoy carnival rides, rodeo, a beer and wine garden, food, pig races, a lumberjack show, a petting zoo, local craft vendors, great music and more! This fair is more fun than it says there are activites for all ages in which children and adults can have fun too! Tickets for rides are needed for this fair. Recommend not to eat a large meal before visiting if you have tickets for the rides.

2.) The Home of Elvis Presley | Graceland

This place is the home of Elvis Presley it's called Graceland. This place is worth your time because there is something for everyone at graceland you can explore like the beautiful mansion, walk the gardens where he found peace, tour the aircraft that he traveled on from show to show, and encounter Elvis Presley's Memphis entertainment complex for an unforgettable experience featuring legendary costumes, artifacts, and personal mementos from Elvis and his family. This tour has tickets which are available on thier official website they also have additional info like ticket prices, their location and the time they are open.

3.) Lover's Leap

This is place is the best place to visit if you want to relax at the sights of a admiring beauty of Tennessee or if you are an exploring type of person. Tennessee is home to some beautiful natural sights, but perhaps the best of the lot can be found at Lover's Leap, which is where you can spot seven different states you can see Seven States. The rare white Fallow deer can also be seen within the Rock City attraction, but for the best views of the land surrounding Lookout Mountain visitors will have to be brave enough to cross the Swing-A-Long bridge which is real scary. From Lover's Leap you will, on a clear day you will be able to see Georgia, Tennessee, Alabama and the Smoky Mountains of North Carolina. If you squint, you might just make out Kentucky and Virginia too also a great test for the eyes. Overall, this place is great for people who love to do some adventuring.
